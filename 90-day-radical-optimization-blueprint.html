The ADHD-ENFP Survival Toolkit

Proven strategies for the specific failure modes your brain is wired for.

‚è± The 2-Minute Bridge

When you feel resistance, commit to only 2 minutes. Your brain's initiation barrier is the bottleneck ‚Äî not your ability. Once you start, momentum carries you 80% of the time. If it doesn't after 2 minutes, switch to a different priority guilt-free.

üéµ Pavlovian Playlists

Assign a specific playlist or album to each work mode (deep focus, creative, admin). Play it ONLY during that mode. Within 2 weeks, your brain will associate the music with the state, making transitions almost automatic.

üëØ Body Doubling

Work alongside someone (in-person or virtually via Focusmate or a co-working stream). The social presence activates accountability circuits your brain needs for non-urgent tasks. This is not a crutch ‚Äî it's a legitimate neurological tool.

üßä Dopamine Menu

Create a written list of healthy dopamine sources organized by effort: low-effort (favorite tea, 5-min walk, a song), medium-effort (call a friend, cook something new), high-effort (workout, creative project). When cravings for cheap dopamine hit, consult the menu first.

ü™§ Friction Engineering

Add friction to bad habits (delete social apps, use app timers, put junk food on a high shelf). Remove friction from good habits (sleep in workout clothes, set up workspace the night before, pre-load your meditation app). Small barriers change everything.

üîÅ The "Never Miss Twice" Rule

Missing one day is human. Missing two is the start of a new pattern. If you miss a workout, the next day's workout is sacred ‚Äî even if it's 10 minutes. This single rule prevents the all-or-nothing spirals ADHD brains are prone to.
